Welcome! I know you're eager to get started suggesting new missions, but please read this page to give you a better chance of success.

How to make a good mission

  • Give your mission a theme, something to link the story of an interesting journey together.
  • Make sure it's not trivially simple or too difficult! Aim for 6-25 waypoints per mission, or around 20-90 minutes.
  • Be sure to make your missions interesting but not too awkward. Just hacks might not be thrilling, but too many capture/mod/link/field actions can be frustrating in enemy territory.
  • Appeal to as many people as possible. Feel free to use your local language!
  • Don't forget to try using passphrases or hidden missions. They can be a lot of fun, and give an extra thrill.
  • Remember: keep your missions safe, and think about the shortest path between your waypoints.

Things to avoid

  • Do not include inappropriate or offensive content, and do not include personally identifiable information.
  • Mission details cannot contain URLs.
  • Please do not use copyrighted images. There are plenty of places to find freely-licensed pictures and icons to use, like Wikimedia Commons and pixabay.
  • Be wary of creating long missions. People will be less likely to complete them if it takes a long time, and being partway through a mission can get in the way of normal gameplay.

Final thoughts

  • It's fine to create biking or driving missions, but please label them as such.
  • Having said that, the original purpose of Ingress was to get people walking around their local communities and discover things they never knew, so try to encourage that.
  • Play fairly. Our review team are not likely to create missions that look like they're griefing/trolling a particular faction or group.
  • Above all, have fun! Sometimes the rules can be bent for specific themes that catch our imaginations.
  • And finally: our review team has members from both factions, and we have agreed to put faction interests aside in order to help the wider community.

Attribution

Please remember that the missions will be created by one of our volunteers. This means that their name and faction will show up as the mission creator in Ingress. This is sadly a limitation of the system, so please bear this in mind. We will aim to include your name in the details of the mission.

If you want a mission to be created by a member of a particular faction (or even a particular reviewer), please note this when submitting your mission.
